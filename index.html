All About Teenage Girl Fashion

Most fashion these days are all about the shoes, clothes and accessories you have and how you put them together. It's all about the colors and how you cordanate them, you have to know what colors look good on you and what colors look good together. The most popular color combanations are, Cherry Red and off White, Baby Blue and White, and Dark Blue and Light Blue. In fashion either your colors have to match or it has to be some kind of vision if the colors are not cordantated. The accessories also play a big part in fashion, gold jewlery only goes with some colors as does silver jewlery.

Clothes

With clothes these days it's all about baggy pants and croped tops, the top clothing choices females have been making recently are, hoodies and leggings, shorts and over-sized shirts, ripped jeans and either a cropped top or over-sized(depends on the girl), there is also pajama pants which in my personal opinion is not really a style its just comfortable and easy to put on. lately people have been going for the Y2K style and dressing like they are from the early 2000s. The most trendy thing these days is to have a hoodie collection.

The Shoes

To be honest it doesn't really matter what shoe you wear as long as they match your outfit and you don't mix certain brands together, so say that you are wearing an Addidas outfit, you can't wear Jordans or Nikes with that. You also have to leern how to protect and take care of you shoes. Keeping your shoes clean i also a thing you see a lot the teenages no one wants their shoes creased, once their shoes get creased they need new ones.

The Hair

When it comes to the hair it is different for different kinds of people, for people with straighter hair or long braids it is very popular to put your hair up with a claw clip. It is very popular to dye your hair. The most popular hair colors right now are, Red, Brown, Blue, Orange, and Blonde.
